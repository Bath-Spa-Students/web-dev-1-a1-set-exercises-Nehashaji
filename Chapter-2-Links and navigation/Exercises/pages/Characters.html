Characters

  1. Belle:A bright and independent young women who dreams of adventure and finding something more than her provincial life.
  2. Beast/Prince AdamThe cursed prince who has been transformed into a fearsome creature and resides in the enchanted castle.
  3. Gaston:A narcissistic hunter who desires Belle as his bride and servers as the film's primary antagonist.
  4. Maurice:Belle's loving and eccentric father who becames lost in the forest and stumbles upon the Beast's castle.
  5. LeFou:Gaston's bumbling sidekick and admirer, providing comedic relief throughout the story.
  6. Mrs. Potts:The maternal teapot who servers as one of the enchanted castle's staff members.
  7. Lumiere:The charming candlestick who is also a member of the enchanted staff.
  8. Cogsworth:The grumpy clock who serves as the castle's major-domo and is always concerned with punctuality.
